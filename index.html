Welcome to your Future.

Alectro is an analytics and technology company working to deliver comprehensive climate- and carbon-related solutions through a complementary agile framework, and Modeling-as-a-Service (MaaS) and Analytics-as-a-Service (AnaaS) projects.

Carbon Analytics

Using our proprietary models, we're able to unlock the potential of the unexplored operations information to create actionable data. We assess GHG Protocol Scope 1, 2 and 3 emissions, create and implement emission reduction plans, and advise on carbon offsets to put you in control of your footprint, and your future.

TCFD Modelling

The Task Force on Climate-related Financial Disclosures (TCFD) aims to help institutions to develop consistent and high-level guidance for assessing and disclosing climate-related risks and opportunities. We help organisations to develop and adopt scenario-based modelling for climate impact assessments.

Internal Carbon Pricing

We can act as a virtual Chief Sustainability Officer to implement best practice innovative Internal Carbon Price (ICP) strategies. ICP gives risks and opportunities a uniform metric to enable financial decision makers to make the low carbon transition an integral part of rational, economic decision making.

Supporting the UN's Climate Neutral Now initiative

Alectro is one of only 21 official global partners to the United Nations Framework Convention on Climate Change's (UNFCCC) initiative, Climate Neutral Now.
Clients can have confidence, through this accreditation, in our Science Based Targets initiative (SBTI) processes, as they have been validated at the highest level.
As part of our work, we can offer companies accreditation from the initiative, connecting organisations globally aiming for the same low-carbon goals.
